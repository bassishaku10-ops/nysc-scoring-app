LOGIN INSTRUCTIONS
• Enter your Officer details carefully before login.
• Ensure your signature is properly drawn and clear.
• Write your correct role before proceeding.
• Only your assigned platoon members who have filled and submitted NYSC Form 2A will be available for scoring after login.
• Do not share login credentials or session access.
• Ensure stable internet connection during scoring.
• The next candidate will not be available for scoring without submitting the present scored candidate.

SCORING NOTES
1. There are 8 headings under which a Corps Member can be scored.
2. Maximum score under headings 1–8 by each Scoring Officer is 10 while minimum is 2 and maximum total score by each Scoring Officer is 80 and minimum is 16.
3. Scoring should be honest, objective and fair. It should be comparative and relative to the group.
